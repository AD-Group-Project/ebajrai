Our Shop:




Description:

Pasar Mini Bajrai is a mini market or grocery shop located in No. 1 Jalan Ampuan, 83000 Batu Pahat, Johor. We provides basic needs by people for their daily life such as food, chicken, meat, sugar, flour, and a variety types of rice, spices and seasonings. Other than that, We also provides frozen foods such as roti canai, donuts and curry puffs.
